About Me

I am a Cybersecurity Analyst with main interest in Cyber Defense, Cyber Threat Intelligence, Open-Source Intelligence Techniques and Social Engineering. I completed my Master’s degree at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology of the Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava, where I pursued Computer Science with a focus on the security of information technologies.

In my spare time, I make the Internet a safer place by reporting application and system vulnerabilities to local and foreign companies.

Apart from cyber security, I have been writing my own songs since 2013. Beside that I like to listen to music, read books and also, I like cycling.

Thank you for visiting my page.
